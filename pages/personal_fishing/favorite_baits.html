Fishing Tips: Bait & Tackle

Explore the best baits and tackle to use for different types of fish and fishing conditions.

Soft Plastics

Perfect for bass fishing in freshwater lakes. Use them with a Texas or Carolina rig for optimal results.

Spinnerbaits

Excellent for targeting active fish in murky water. Best used during overcast days or low-light conditions.

Crankbaits

Ideal for covering large areas quickly. Great for mid-depth fishing and attracting predatory fish.

Live Bait

Effective for almost all species. Worms, minnows, and leeches work particularly well for beginners.

Fly Fishing Tackle

Use artificial flies for trout and salmon in streams and rivers. A must-have for fly fishing enthusiasts.
